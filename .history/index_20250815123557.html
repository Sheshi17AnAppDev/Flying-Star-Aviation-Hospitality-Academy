Certified Ground Staff Professionals

Duration: 11 Months (5 Days a week / 2 Hours a day)

Eligibility: 12 pass

Aspiring ground staffers need to be friendly, calm, and emphatic towards customers' problems... They should be prepared to deal with different passengers with a smile, while always being ready to help travellers in need. As part of their role, they are required to solve the passengers' queries, provide flight information, and ensure a trouble-free flight journey. Ground staffs form the backbone of the aviation industry. While computers and modernization can streamline various processes, the task performed by ground staff cannot be replicated by any machine.

Certified Cabin Crew Professionals

Duration: 9 -11 Months (5 Days a week / 2 Hours a day)

Eligibility: 12 pass

Working as a cabin crew can be an exciting experience as well as a challenging one... While people only see the exotic destinations cabin crew gets to travel to, they don't see the amount of hard work and effort put in by them to manage the huge responsibility of passengers' safety and comfort throughout the journey. Cabin crew members spend months of training in acquiring industry-aligned specialization skills. These skills become vital when they have to serve these passengers thousands of feet above the ground for many hours in the day.
